Astra

Astra harnesses the energies of the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With full command of her astral form and a talent for deep strategic foresight, she’s always eons ahead of her enemy’s next move.


Brimstone

Brimstone is a powerful commander that utilizes incendiary grenades, smokescreen airstrikes, and rapid-fire-inducing stim beacons to damage enemies and buff teammates.


Breach

Breach is a beast of an initiator with abilities that can go through terrain to deal damage and CC enemies. He has the most crowd control with abilities that can blind, knockup, and disorient.
